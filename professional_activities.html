Professional Activities
I have been invited to contribute to 10 international conferences and workshops as publication chair, demonstration chair, proceedings chair, publicity chair, or track chair. I have also served as a Proceeding Editor for four conferences:
  • 7th Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) International Conference on Data Science and Advanced Analytics (DSAA 2020)
  • 13th International Conference on Advanced Data Mining and Applications (ADMA 2017)
  • 5th and 6th Australasian Symposium on Service Research and Innovation (ASSRI 2015, 2017), published by Springer.

I have joined the Technical Program Committee of >30 international conferences in the fields of artificial intelligence, Web technologies, and data analytics such as AAAI. IJCAI, WWW, ACL, EMNLP, KDD, SDM, ICSOC, WISE

I am serving in Editorial Board for Natural Language Processing Journal. I am also Editor of special issue in Future Internet. I have served as a Guest Editor for special issue in Personal and Ubiquitous Computing. I am also regularly asked to act as a referee for many international journals, including ACM Transactions of Information Systems, Pattern Recognition, ACM Computing Surveys , IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, Knowledge Based Systems, and Journal of Association for Information Science and Technology.
